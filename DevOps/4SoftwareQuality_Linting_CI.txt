One DevOps Definition:
CALMS
C = Culture (Shared Responsibiity, Continous Improvement, Psykologisk sikkerhed)
A = Automation (Eliminate manual work)
L = Lean (Small incrmenets of working software to end users)
M = Measuring, Monitorering ()
S = Sharing (Transparency, Visibility and knowledge sharing)
